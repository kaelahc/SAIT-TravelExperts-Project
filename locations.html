Japan
Experience traditional Japanese culture at this historic Ryokan. This well-appointed Ryokan has over 150 years of history and is regularly selected as the top Ryokan in Japan. Each room is styled in the traditional Japanese manner with tatami floors, a beautiful laquered table, and cozy futons. Guests can choose a room with a private onsen (hot spring) or use the main onsen with other guests, gender segregated for comfort. The food will wow you with an artisan selection of Japanese-style keiseki (course) dinners, full of fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Starting at $400 CAD/night
Flights to Kyoto from $800 CAD
